How We Work

At Bright Hygiene how we work has two key focus areas – safety and quality.

These underpin how we continually maintain and improve our training and management systems throughout the company. They ensure that not only do we have continuous monitoring processes in place but also that we regularly review and improve our systems and procedures based on our findings. We have an excellent track record in promoting the health, safety and wellbeing of everyone who could be impacted by our work – from our cleaning operatives, to chefs, facilities managers, restaurant owners and their customers. The safe use of cleaning chemicals, the safe operation of specialist cleaning equipment, and the quality of our deep cleaning ensures that how we work upholds the highest industry standards. We haven’t achieved this alone. Bright Hygiene has always worked with our clients to ensure that we also work within their policies and best practices in line with our internal safety methods.
