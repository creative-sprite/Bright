Sensitive Locations

The service team members, as well as the surveyors and auditors of Bright Hygiene have experience in carrying out deep cleans in an assortment of environments including the most sensitive areas located in the following facilities:

  1. Hospitals
  2. Military sites
  3. Police Stations
  4. Manufacturing
  5. Airports
  6. Prisons
  7. Government Buildings

Our cleaning technicians hold enhanced DBS certificates, meaning they can clear vetting at high-security sites and giving our clients peace of mind that our staff can be trusted in areas which may hold sensitive information.
